== Description ==

The Stripe Payment Gateway allows you to accept payments on your wordpress site by integrating WooCommerce and your Stripe account. You can perform card authorizations, automatic settlement, refunds, and much more. For those who whish to 
accept donations on their site this plugin includes a donation feature.

= Features =
- SAQ A PCI compliant
- Beautifully designed forms out of the box
- Integrates with Woocommerce
- Integrates with Woocommerce Subscriptions 2.0.0+
- Integrations with WooCommerce currency switchers
- Accept credit cards, Alipay, & Bitcoin.
- Accept donations
- Authorize & capture or automatically settle
- Custom Order Metadata
- Issue refunds
- Dynamic descriptors
- Customers can save payment methods.
- Change subscription payment methods.
- Manage payment methods
